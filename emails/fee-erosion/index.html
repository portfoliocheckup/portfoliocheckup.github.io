Did you know that if you could reduce your fees by just 1%, your money could last you 10 years longer in retirement.

Imagine 3 friends all have $100,000 to invest at age 35 and each are fortunate enough to average 8% annual returns over their lifetime. However, they are paying 1%, 2% and 3% respectively in annual fees. The one who pays 1% in fees will have approx. $761,000 at age 65 while the one paying 3% in fees has just $432,000.

Same investment amount, same investment returns, but one has 44% less in their retirement account!

And when the time comes to retire, by taking out the same $65,000 per year, the friend who has been paying just 1% in fees, will have his money last to age 95, while his friend who pays 3% in fees will run out of money before age 75.
